Hear Cultivating Local Yokals' Executive Director, Richard Gauthier, tell his story in a new documentary from Andy Fiore, "Invisible No More."

From the website:

Unseen and unheard, a substantial subset of the incarcerated population quietly grapples with undiagnosed traumatic brain injuries. This powerful and unique documentary resonates with current global trends in brain injury and criminal justice and explores the intricate interplay between them.

Learn more about the documentary at invisiblenomore.ca.
