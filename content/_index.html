Our society's main function is to provide a safe environment, which is a garden, and facilitate programs for children with brain injury. We have been using this type of venue for over 12 years, finding that programs in a friendly garden setting are much more effective in the re-learning process. We find that the children are much more relaxed in this type of environment, and respond well to outdoor programs.

— Richard Gauthier, Executive Director
Cultivating Local Yokals Society ---
